About Me

Hey there, I'm Jason Gianakopulos.

My journey has been anything but conventional. Skipping the traditional high school route, I faced challenges that sparked an unconventional path of self-discovery and learning.

My fascination with computers, programming, and cybersecurity became the driving force behind my self-taught education. Starting in 2012, I dove deeper into the complexities of coding, gradually shifting my focus toward the ethical dimensions of cybersecurity. What may seem like setbacks have, in reality, been stepping stones, fueling my commitment to overcoming obstacles with unyielding curiosity.

Today, I'm all about applying my skills for positive change and embracing the principles of whitehat professionalism. Despite lacking formal education and traditional certifications, I've come to realize the transformative power of practical knowledge and a steadfast dedication to ethical cybersecurity practices.

Unraveling the intricacies of code and navigating the ever-evolving landscape of cybersecurity brings me genuine joy. Each challenge is an opportunity to learn and grow, and I'm on a perpetual journey to stay ahead in technology while upholding the highest ethical standards in my work. I believe in the human side of technology, where resilience, curiosity, and a commitment to ethical principles drive meaningful progress.
